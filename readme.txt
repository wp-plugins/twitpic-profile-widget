This widget will display your Twitpic Profile in the widget area displaying your latest twitpics.

== Description ==

This widget will display your Twitpic Profile Widget with up to 20 photos on your site. 

== Installation ==

1. Sign up for Twitter at [http://twitter.com/](http://twitter.com/ "Twitter").
2. Sign up for Twitpic at [http://twitpic.com/](http://twitpic.com/ "Twitpic").
3. Go to Appearance -> Widgets -> Drag Twitpic Profile Widget to your widget area.
4. Set your Twitter username and adjust the other widget settings to your liking and you're done.
